Documentation

How it works

The logic behind every number on your dashboard.

Core formulas

Daily kWh    = (Watts × Hours/day) ÷ 1000
Daily cost    = Daily kWh × ₹/kWh rate
Monthly kWh  = Daily kWh × Days/month
Monthly bill  = Monthly kWh × ₹/kWh rate
Yearly bill    = Monthly bill × 12
CO₂ (grams)  = kWh × 820
Eff. rate      = Bill amount ÷ Units consumed

Carbon emission factor

India’s grid emission factor is 820 grams of CO₂ per kWh — the national average from the Central Electricity Authority (CEA).
